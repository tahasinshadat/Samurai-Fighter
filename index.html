This Game is not compatible with mobile devices, please go on a computer to play this game!

How To Play:

Controls:

Player 1 Controls (Ronin):
  • W - Jump
  • A - Move Left
  • D - Move Right
  • SPACE - Attack
Player 2 Controls (Hiroshi):
  • UP ARROW - Jump
  • LEFT ARROW - Move Left
  • RIGHT ARROW - Move Right
  • SHIFT - Attack

Scenario:

Ronin and Hiroshi, two legendary samurai, cross blades on the moonlit rooftop of Edo. Ronin seeks redemption for a past dishonor, while Hiroshi's ambition drives him. The clash of swords reflects their conflicting paths, as fate weaves a tale of honor and power, destined to be settled under the night sky.

  • Player 1 (Ronin) has a higher attack damage due to focus
  • Player 2 (Hiroshi) has a higher attack speed due to ferocity

How to Win:

  • Best 2 of 3 win system
  • Attack the other player until dead
  • Have the most health before round is over

Game Designs

Attacking & Health Depletion Logic
  • Player 1 does more damage because his attack animation is longer so he has a slower attack speed
  • Player 2 does less damage because his attack animation is shorter meaning he has a faster attack speed

Thus:

  • Player 1 takes 6 FULL ATTACKS to kill Player 2
  • Player 2 takes 7 FULL ATTACKS to kill Player 1
Semi-Attacks:
  • Semi-Attacks are when the player or other player moves outside of the attack hit range mid attack
  • This leads to the attack doing only a certain PERCENTAGE of the full damage

Example:

  • SCENARIO: Player 1 attacks and Player 2 leaves Player 1's attack range after 75% of the animation
  • RESULT: Player 2 will recieve 75% of the damage Player 1 can output
